JNLink Logistic Desktop App

Surya Jaya Niaga is a micro-medium enterprise that operates in the logistics sector. Surya Jaya Niaga provides various logistics needs, such as delivery vehicles which have various types and can be used for various needs as well as providing drivers to send goods to their destination according to requests from customers.

Description

Based on the problems faced by Surya Jaya Niaga, an information system is needed that is able to shorten, make it more effective and automate several processes that are still manual-based through the creation of a desktop logistics transportation management system application. With this application, it is hoped that the raw data transformation process can be managed into graphs or charts that can be used for further analysis processes to reach business decisions. Then, with this application it is hoped that the automation process for daily maintenance and transactions as well as weekly and monthly reports can be recorded and entered and visualized automatically in the application. Then, it is also hoped that this application can help Surya Jaya Niaga to evaluate or determine future decisions regarding fleet procurement for vehicle types based on vehicle rental frequency and also help input data from customers who have collaborative relationships or business partners.
